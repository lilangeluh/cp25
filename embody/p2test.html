Assembly sheet

  1. step 01
    Attach the four legs to the underside of the base panel. All feet should point in the same direction.
    drawing for step 01 (legs)
  2. step 02
    Place the seat panel across the legs and fasten it with the long screws, working diagonally so the load spreads evenly.
    drawing for step 02 (seat)
  3. step 03
    Slide the backrest pieces into their sockets on the seat. Press down until you feel them settle.
    drawing for step 03 (backrest)
  4. step 04
    Walk around the chair and tighten every joint. Stop when the wood feels firm, not strained.
    drawing for step 04 (final check)
